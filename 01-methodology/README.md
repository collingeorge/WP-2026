## Document Summaries

### ATTRIBUTION-01 — The Nexus Protocol
Four-level state nexus attribution framework: Presence, Linkage, Support, Direct Tasking. Defines disconfirmation criteria, corroboration standards, and institutional calibration across OFAC, DOJ, State, IC, and Commerce/BIS. Six attribution failure modes including advocacy capture and toleration-as-tasking. All series documents that make attribution claims import from this framework by cross-reference.

### NEXUS-01 / CSE-01 — The PRC State Nexus Protocol
Extends ATTRIBUTION-01 with a fifth rung (Enablement) for PRC commercial state nexus analysis. Adds beneficial ownership analysis, VIE architecture treatment, OFAC 50 Percent Rule, FinCEN CDD, and CFIUS effective-control test. Four Support/Enablement boundary tests. Seven failure modes. Required reading before any PRC-facing enforcement analysis.

### AML-01 v2.2 — AML/CFT/CPF Control Matrix
Bank-grade, policy-grade control matrix mapping 20 node types across nine illicit finance categories. Eight-column structure: documented exposure pattern, risk indicators, controls, escalation indicators, reporting pathways, statutory authority, FATF alignment, and enforcement basis. Three appendices: analytic posture framework, control typology classification, false-positive discipline. Three-pass red-team statutory audit completed (statutory precision, escalation language, enforcement exposure). Fully compliant with BSA/FinCEN regulations, FATF Recommendations, OFAC compliance frameworks, and AMLA 2020.

### SENI-02 v2.2 — Named-Entity Enforcement Index
Named-entity companion to AML-01. 65+ institutions organized by AML-01 node category. Seven-column structure including Status classification (A=Active/D=Designated/I=Indicted/R=Reported/H=Historical/O=Ongoing) and statutory authority basis per row. Every entry attribution-first, time-bounded, source-status differentiated. Three red-team passes applied.

### SCORES-01 — Official CBO, JCT, and GAO Fiscal Scores
Compilation and classification of publicly available fiscal estimates from CBO, JCT, SSA, and GAO across five policy families: revenue measures, entitlement reform, defense, discretionary spending, and tax administration/enforcement. Defines and applies a four-category classification vocabulary:

- **★ Official legislative score** — CBO or JCT score of enacted or introduced legislation under statutory scorekeeping authority
- **◆ Official budget-option estimate** — CBO/JCT *Options for Reducing the Deficit* compendium or comparable institutional publication; not a score of specific legislation
- **◆ Institutional modeling exercise** — CBO working paper, SSA actuarial analysis, or comparable institutional modeling of a hypothetical policy design
- **◇ Illustrative planning range** — author synthesis of available estimates; not independently scored; should not be presented to a committee, budget office, or scoring authority as a scored estimate

SCORES-01 is the series' authoritative source on what category any given fiscal estimate belongs to and how it may be cited. All series documents that cite fiscal figures verify against SCORES-01 categorization. Reads into FISCAL-01 (`03-strategic-architecture`) and TARIFF-01 (`03-strategic-architecture`) as their evidentiary reference foundation.

## Series Position

Documents in this folder are the methodological foundation layer. They own their respective analytical domains exclusively — no other series document may independently analyze state nexus attribution, illicit finance node architecture, entity-level enforcement status, or fiscal estimate classification at equivalent depth.

Reading order within this folder: ATTRIBUTION-01 → NEXUS-01 → AML-01 → SENI-02 → SCORES-01. SCORES-01 should be read before FISCAL-01 and TARIFF-01 in `03-strategic-architecture`.
