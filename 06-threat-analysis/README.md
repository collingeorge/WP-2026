## Series Position

HORMUZ-01 and HORMUZ-02 read upstream from BRI-01 (`03-strategic-architecture`) and EST-01 (`03-strategic-architecture`). The Hormuz crisis context — IRGC declared closure, March 2, 2026 — is the active operational environment against which the BRI-01 corridor wager and EST-01 strategic estimate are conditioned.

The Iran nuclear and WMD assessments are standalone threat products. They do not read into the enforcement framework stack but provide the threat environment context for proliferation finance targeting in SENI-01 (`04-applied-enforcement`) and CPF-02 (`10-under-revision`).

---

## Analytical Standards

All documents in this folder apply dual-axis confidence labeling (probability of assessment × confidence in sourcing). Assessments are time-bound to the period specified. No classified or controlled information was used or implied.
