## MDO-01 v3.0 — Paper

**Multi-Domain Operations Doctrine: Strategic Framework, Execution Architecture, and the Three-Track Analytic Model — Final Edition**

Eight parts covering joint warfighting doctrine, five-domain architecture, the three-track analytic model (Diplomatic / Overt Warfare / Clandestine/Covert), PSYOP and information operations, SOF and covert action, adversary context (PRC / Russia / DPRK), F2T2EA targeting workflow, and structural constraints. ODNI two-axis confidence standard throughout. Three revision passes: v1 (initial), v2 (red-team structural revision), v3 (A+ polish).

**Key Judgments:** MDO almost certainly governs joint warfighting for peer conflict (High Confidence / Doctrine-Supported). Three-track framework is an analytic organizing construct, not a codified decision architecture (Moderate Confidence / Analytically Inferred). All six KJs carry stated basis, principal counterargument, and reassessment conditions.

**Reads into:** HORMUZ-01/02, SHIELD-01, PERSIST-01, MAXPRESS-01

## MDO-01 Analytic Console — Interface

**Decision-support interface operationalizing MDO-01 v3.0. Does not determine, recommend, or prioritize courses of action.**

Ten interactive modules:

- **Overview** — six key judgments with expandable analytical sub-elements
- **Three-Track Comparator** — side-by-side T1/T2/T3 across seven dimensions
- **Authorities and Friction Map** — legal predicate, approval burden, documented friction
- **Adversary Context** — PRC / Russia / DPRK stress test with track modification notes
- **Campaign Workflow** — four-phase viewer with escalation triggers and off-ramps
- **Confidence Matrix** — ten analytic claims with basis, counterargument, reassessment conditions
- **F2T2EA Targeting** — six-phase sequence with decision conditions and legal gates
- **Case Validation** — three bounded sanctions mini-cases (Iran / Russia / DPRK)
- **Scenario Builder** — condition-based categorical viability assessment (scenario-conditioned outputs)
- **Export Brief** — structured analytic snapshot with methodological limit block

**Governance architecture:** Identity band, scenario-conditioned badge, no-synthesis declaration, module separation, categorical viability system (no pseudo-quantification), methodological limit block on all brief outputs.

**CI Assessment:** WP-2026-CI-PLATFORM-01 (in `00-series-standards/`).
